Program Overview

ReAttire is a structured career transformation program that converts military experience into corporate success through specialized training, mentorship, and placement support.

Month 1: Foundation Program

An intensive foundation covering:

  • Corporate Culture & Mindset
  • Professional Communication
  • Digital & AI Literacy
  • Career Pathway Exploration

Month 2: Specialization Track

Deep dive into your chosen field:

  • HR & Administration
  • Operations Management
  • Sales & Business Development
  • Project Management

Month 3: Career Launch

Final phase ensuring job readiness:

  • Industry Internship
  • Interview Preparation
  • Resume & Portfolio Building
  • Placement Support
