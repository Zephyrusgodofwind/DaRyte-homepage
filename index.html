Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of startups do you fund?

We fund early-stage startups working on dual-use and defense-specific technologies, including but not limited to: autonomous systems (UAVs, UGVs), AI/ML for battlefield intelligence, cybersecurity, secure communications, advanced materials, and space technology.

How much equity do you take?

Our standard deal is for 6-8% equity in exchange for seed funding up to â‚¹2 Crore, access to our secure facilities, intensive mentorship from defense experts, and our unparalleled government and industry network. Terms can be flexible based on the startup's stage and needs.

Is the program residential?

While the program is not strictly residential, we require founders to spend significant time on-site at our secure facilities in New Delhi. This is crucial for leveraging our R&D labs, mentorship sessions, and collaborative opportunities with defense personnel.

Who can apply?

We welcome applications from teams and founders registered in India. We look for deep technical expertise, a clear understanding of the defense problem they are solving, and a prototype or MVP that demonstrates their innovative approach.
